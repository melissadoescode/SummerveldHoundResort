A Bit About Us ...

Who are we?

We are a family operated animal rescue. Our NPO (NPO 225-939) does not receive government or municipal funding, we rely solely on donations from the public.

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue and rehabilitate neglected, abused and unwanted animals. We also aim to raise awareness and promote the welfare of all animals.

How we operate

We are a forever home to our rescues because we believe that they are part of our family. We provide our rescues with a safe haven & a better quality of life in a family environment.

Why we don't rehome

We did attempt to rehome some of our rescues numerous times, homechecks were even done & approved, but during our surprise visits we were appalled at seeing their conditions.
